Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I'm suitable for your life coaching program?

We offer a complimentary session where we delve into your specific needs through a series of insightful questions. This detailed exploration helps us understand your goals and challenges, ensuring our program aligns perfectly with your journey.

How long does a typical coaching engagement last?

Each situation is unique, but from our experience, a foundational course typically includes 8 one-on-one sessions. This structure allows us to comprehensively address your needs and guide you towards your goals.

What makes BizPro Coaching different from other coaching services?
I’ve uniquely designed this program specifically for professionals facing the challenges of an imbalanced life. Having been through similar experiences myself, I know what it means to seek genuine support. The difference at BizPro Coaching is our integrity and dedication—if I feel that I can’t help you, I will honestly recommend another coach who can better meet your needs.
